  Discovering events on Whim feels effortless. Every card is a real thing happening near you — posted by students, for students.   - Filter by distance, your school, or category to find what fits your vibe
  - Join an event group chat the moment you swipe right
  - See which of your friends are already going before you commit
  - Create your own event in seconds and post it to your campus feed

  FOR COLLEGE STUDENTS ONLY
  Whim is exclusively for university students. Sign up with your .edu email and connect with people who actually go to your school.

  ALWAYS IN THE KNOW
  Whim shows you what's happening close to you, sorted by how soon it starts. Live events surface to the top so you never miss something going on right now.
