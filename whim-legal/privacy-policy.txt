Profile Information:

Social and Safety Data:
- Friend requests sent and received
- Accepted friendship connections
- Users you have blocked
- Reports you submit about other users (including reason and description)

3.2 Information We Collect Automatically

Location Information:
We collect precise geolocation data (GPS coordinates) in the following specific circumstances:
   (a) Event Creation: When you create an event and choose to use your current location, we collect your precise GPS coordinates to populate the event's location and coordinates.
   (b) Attendance Check-In: When you use the in-app check-in feature, we collect your precise GPS coordinates at the moment of check-in to verify that you are physically within approximately 150 meters of the event location. This location data is transmitted to our servers, processed to determine verification status, and a boolean result (verified/not verified) is stored. Your precise check-in coordinates are processed by our secure edge function and are used solely for the purpose of attendance verification.
   (c) Background Location: On iOS and Android devices, we may request "background location" permission to enable check-in reminder notifications and to allow you to trigger check-in verification without opening the app. We do not continuously track or record your location in the background beyond what is necessary for these specific functions.

   Important: Your precise real-time GPS coordinates are NOT shared with other Whim users. Other users may see that you have attended or checked in to an event, but they cannot see your GPS coordinates.

Device and Usage Information:
- Device operating system and version
- App version
- Push notification token (Expo push token, a device-specific identifier used solely for delivering push notifications)
- Session and authentication tokens (stored securely on-device using Expo SecureStore)
- App interaction data, such as events viewed, swiped, or joined

Log Data:
- Internet Protocol (IP) address
- Browser type and version (for website access)
- Pages viewed and time spent
- Access timestamps
- Error logs and crash reports

6. LOCATION DATA — DETAILED DISCLOSURE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Location data is among the most sensitive data we collect. This section provides a detailed explanation of our location data practices.

6.1 What Location Data We Collect

We collect precise GPS coordinates (latitude and longitude) in two specific contexts:
   (a) Event Coordinates: When you create an event using your current location, your coordinates at that moment are stored as the event's coordinates. These are visible to authenticated users in proximity to the event.
   (b) Check-In Verification: When you check in to an event, your device transmits your current GPS coordinates to our servers. Our system computes whether you are within 150 meters of the event. Only the verification result (true/false) is permanently stored; your raw check-in coordinates are processed and not permanently stored in our database.

6.2 What Location Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do not continuously track your location
- We do not build a history of your physical movements
- We do not share your real-time location with other users
- We do not use your location for advertising purposes

6.3 Background Location Permission

On iOS and Android, we may request permission to access your location while the app is in the background ("always on" location permission). We use this permission only to:
   (a) Send you timely check-in reminder notifications when you are near an event you joined
   (b) Allow you to trigger check-in verification without manually opening the app

You can revoke background location permission in your device settings at any time. Revoking this permission may limit your ability to receive timely check-in reminders.

6.4 Legal Basis (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area, we process location data on the basis of your consent, which you provide when granting location permissions on your device, and where necessary for the performance of the contract between you and Whim (i.e., providing the Service's check-in and event creation features).

7. DATA RETENTION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

We retain your personal information for different periods depending on the type of data and its purpose:

Event Listings and Messages:
Events and their associated group chat messages are automatically deleted approximately 24 hours after the event's scheduled end time via our automated cleanup process. This means the content of event chats is not retained after events expire.

Attendance Records (Check-Ins):
Records of verified event attendance (event_checkins) are retained indefinitely as part of your profile's attendance history. This data is used to calculate and display your "show rate" statistic.

Profile Data:
Your profile information (name, university, major, graduation year, clubs, photos) is retained until you delete your account.

Friend Connections and Social Data:
Friend relationships and block records are retained until you remove them or delete your account.

Push Notification Tokens:
Your device's push notification token is retained until your account is deleted or you update the token.

Authentication Data:
Email address and authentication credentials are retained until account deletion, subject to any mandatory legal retention requirements.

Bug and User Reports:
Reports you submit about bugs or other users are retained by our administrative team for safety and compliance purposes, even after account deletion, to ensure the integrity of our moderation process.

Account Deletion:
When you delete your account, we initiate a permanent deletion of your profile and associated personal data, including your profile information, events you created, messages you sent, friendships, blocks, attendance records, and push tokens. Certain anonymized or aggregated data derived from your activity may be retained for statistical purposes. Some data may be retained for a limited period as required by law or to resolve disputes.

10. DATA SECURITY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. These measures include:

- Row-Level Security (RLS) policies on all database tables, ensuring users can only access data they are authorized to see
- Encrypted authentication using Supabase's secure authentication infrastructure
- HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data transmitted between your device and our servers
- Secure, hashed password storage (plaintext passwords are never stored)
- Access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel
- Automated event cleanup removing sensitive chat data after events expire
- Secure local token storage on devices using Expo SecureStore

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it to security@joinwhim.net.
