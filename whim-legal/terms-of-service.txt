3.2 Event Discovery. The Service provides a swipe-based interface to discover and join events posted by other users within configurable distance parameters. Events are displayed based on proximity to your device's location, relevance, and time.

   (d) The event complies with all applicable laws, campus rules, and these Terms.

3.4 Event Attendance and Check-In. When you join an event, you become a member of that event's attendee list and associated group chat. By using the check-in feature, you consent to the collection and processing of your precise GPS location at the time of check-in solely for the purpose of verifying attendance.

3.5 Event Chat. Joining an event grants you access to a group chat for that event. Messages sent in event chats are visible to all attendees of that event. Messages are retained until the event is automatically archived (generally within 24 hours after the event time). Once posted, messages cannot be edited or deleted by users. You are solely responsible for the content of messages you send.

3.6 Social Features. The Service includes features to send and receive friend requests, view other users' public profiles, and see mutual connections. These features are intended to facilitate real-world social interactions in connection with events.
